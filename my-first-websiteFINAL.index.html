  2. The Exorcist (1973)
  3. Halloween (1978)
  4. Nightmare on Elm Street (1984)
  5. The Thing (1982)
  6. The Shining (1980)
  7. The Evil Dead (1981)
  8. The Blair Witch Project (1999)
  9. The Silence of the Lambs (1991)
  10. Friday the 13th (1980)
